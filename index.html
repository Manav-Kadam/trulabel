Blazing Fast Image Labeling Tool

TruLabel is an image labeling tool designed for optimum productivity. Keyboard shortcuts, interface layout, collaboration, everything is designed to build the most accurate datasets for machine learning.

Own your data and algorithms

TruLabel image annotation tool does not try to own your data or algorithms, but integrates with them seamlessly. No duplicate source of truth and complicated scripts to synchronize your data between various tools

Open community and standard

TruLabel is building a community around an open source data labeling tool (under Business Source Licence) to set the standard around visual data management. Data cleaning should not require any “secret sauce”.

Integrate TruLabel image labeling tool into your own tech stack

You can get started on TruLabel in a few seconds, by uploading your images in the app to start annotating images right away

But TruLabel can also connect to your own data stack allowing you to stay 100% in control of your data and algorithms . TruLabel backend is open-source and you can customize it to integrate all your tools around your data stack. No duplicate source of truth and complicated scripts to synchronize your data between various tools. Managing large datasets for machine learning is now made easy.
